Welcome to Moreish


Moreish is a prominent brand in Bread and Bakery products in Eastern India. For 3 decades our freshly and passionately baked products are reaching out to millions of households daily.


Moreish Foods Limited was established in Ranchi, and Moreish has today grown into Eastern India's largest bread manufacturing company. Moreish bread, has grown into a premium well-known and most preferred brand in Jharkhand and Bihar. Moreish adheres to stringent measures in all aspects of manufacturing, raw material sourcing and packaging.

"Freshly packed loaf when baked with love, brings the world together on the table."
